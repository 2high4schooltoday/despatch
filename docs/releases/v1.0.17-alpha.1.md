# Alpha 1.0.17

Mail workflow and account-management release for the Alpha 1.0 line.

## Included

- Simplifies sending setup with first-class sender profiles, clearer account/default controls, and security-only browser sessions instead of mixed session/signature/identity behavior.
- Adds a dedicated Contacts workspace with people and groups, nicknames, CSV/VCF import-export, and richer compose autocomplete that prefers saved contacts over mail-history guesses.
- Adds a Rules & Junk area in Mail settings, managed per-account Sieve rules, and first-class `Spam` / `Not Spam` actions in the mail UI.
- Adds a Mail `Account Health` panel with sync freshness, last error, quota visibility, and per-account `Retry Sync`, `Refresh Quota`, and `Rebuild Index` actions.
- Fixes core mail reliability around preview cleanup, reader inspect/source routing, scheduled send, search parsing, trash/folder moves, and conversation threading for both new replies and repaired older indexed messages.
